Privacy policy

Privacy at Internet of Growth

We collect only the information we need to operate, improve, and provide access to Internet of Growth. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices available to you.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Who we are

Internet of Growth is a closed-loop decision intelligence platform for Direct-to-Consumer brands, powered by Outcome Machines. In this policy, "Internet of Growth," "we," "us," and "our" refer to the team operating this website and related early-access services.

2. Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Contact details, such as your name, work email address, and company name.
  • Brand and company details, such as company size, revenue range, commerce platform, and growth priorities.
  • Messages you send to us, including support requests, partnership inquiries, or early-access responses.

We may also collect technical information automatically when you visit the site, such as device type, browser type, referring page, approximate location derived from IP address, pages viewed, and interaction events.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Respond to early-access requests and communicate with prospective customers.
  • Understand which DTC brands may be a fit for design partnerships or product pilots.
  • Operate, secure, debug, and improve the website and related services.
  • Develop product strategy, research market needs, and improve our messaging.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights where necessary.

4. Forms and third-party services

We use Tally to embed and process the early-access form on this site. Information submitted through that form is processed by Tally on our behalf and is subject to Tally's own privacy and security practices.

We may also use service providers for hosting, analytics, email, security, customer communication, and operational tooling. These providers are permitted to process information only as needed to provide their services to us.

5. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies, pixels, local storage, or similar technologies to keep the site working, understand aggregate usage, measure campaign performance, and improve the visitor experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some site functionality may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.

6. How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information:

  • With vendors and service providers that help us operate the website and early-access process.
  • With professional advisors, such as lawyers, accountants, or security consultants.
  • To comply with law, legal process, or valid government requests.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of Internet of Growth, our users, or others.
  • In connection with a financing, merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets.

7. Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including operating early access, maintaining business records, resolving disputes, and complying with legal obligations. If you ask us to delete your information, we will take reasonable steps to do so unless we need to retain it for legitimate business or legal reasons.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, or objection related to your personal information. You may also opt out of non-essential communications from us at any time.

To make a request, contact us at hello@internetofgrowth.com. We may need to verify your request before acting on it.

10. International visitors

Internet of Growth is operated from the United States. If you access the site from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate.

11. Children's privacy

Internet of Growth is intended for business users and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be communicated through reasonable means where appropriate.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy or our privacy practices can be sent to hello@internetofgrowth.com.
